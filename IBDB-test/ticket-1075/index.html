Description:

"This is Broadway" was a series of short radio interviews with Broadway luminaries conducted by Isobel Robins and Richard Seff. The interviews, produced by The League of New York Theatres and Producers (now the Broadway League), were used to promote Broadway circa 1977 and syndicated to radio stations around the country. They appear here through a collaboration between The Broadway League and the American Theatre Wing.
